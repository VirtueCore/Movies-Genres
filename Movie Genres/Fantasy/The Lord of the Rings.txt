The Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring (2001)

In the mythical land of Middle-earth, a humble hobbit named Frodo Baggins inherits a powerful ring that could bring ruin to the world. Joined by a fellowship of allies, he begins a perilous journey to destroy the One Ring before the dark lord Sauron can reclaim it. Known for epic world-building and timeless themes of courage and friendship.
